
Attribute 1 : Number of reviews on stadiums, sports complex, etc. 
Attribute 2 : Number of reviews on religious institutions 
Attribute 3 : Number of reviews on beach, lake, river, etc. 
Attribute 4 : Number of reviews on theatres, exhibitions, etc. 
Attribute 5 : Number of reviews on malls, shopping places, etc. 
Attribute 6 : Number of reviews on parks, picnic spots, etc.



This dataset was populated from destination reviews published by 249 reviewers of an online touristic web site. 
Reviews falling in 6 categories and the count of reviews in each category for every reviewer (traveller) is recorded.
